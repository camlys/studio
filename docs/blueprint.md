# **App Name**: ChronoFlow

## Core Features:

- Manual Date Input & Validation: User-friendly input fields for Day, Month, and Year with smart validation, auto-formatting, placeholder examples (DD, MM, YYYY), auto-cursor movement to the next field, and prevention of invalid or future dates.
- Age Calculation: Accurately calculate and display the user's exact age in various units (years, months, weeks, days, hours, minutes, seconds) based on their entered date of birth, including leap year handling.
- Date Difference Calculator: Toggle to a mode that calculates the duration between any two user-entered dates (From and To), showing the difference in multiple units.
- Next Birthday & Zodiac Display: Show a countdown to the user's next birthday and their astrological zodiac sign based on their date of birth.
- Theming & Utility Controls: Implement a dark/light mode toggle for preferred viewing, along with a copy/share result button and a reset button for quick input clearing.
- Interactive Result Display: Dynamically animated cards to display calculation results clearly, featuring total days lived and detailed time breakdowns.
- Fun Age Facts Generator: Utilize an AI tool to generate a fun, engaging, and relevant fact about the user's age or a general age-related tidbit.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Luminous Cyan (#4CCFFF), chosen for a modern, tech-inspired, and professional feel, providing a clean contrast against dark backgrounds.
- Background color: Deep Blue-Gray (#1F2629), serving as a dark, desaturated base that allows for glassmorphism effects and neon glows to stand out subtly.
- Accent color: Vibrant Mint Green (#66FFCC), analogous to the primary color, used for interactive elements, highlights, and subtle neon glow accents.
- Primary font: 'Inter' (sans-serif), chosen for its modern, clean, and objective aesthetic, ideal for displaying numerical data and ensuring readability across all text elements.
- Modern, minimalist, and geometric line icons or subtly gradiented duotone icons will be used to complement the glassmorphism design and maintain a premium feel.
- Professional, mobile-first card-based layout featuring rounded cards with glassmorphism effects (translucency and blur), soft shadows for depth, and an animated gradient background for dynamic visual appeal. A sticky, modern navigation bar ensures easy access to main features.
- Smooth micro-interactions on hover and focus states for input fields and buttons. Results will be presented with elegant animated transitions, and content within result cards will animate dynamically for an engaging user experience. The background will feature a subtle, continuously animated gradient.
